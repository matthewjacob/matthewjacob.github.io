Welcome! I am a PhD student in Public Policy (Economics Track) at Harvard University.

My research interests are in public and labor economics, with a focus on economic mobility, social capital, and inequality.

Prior to graduate school, I was a Pre-Doctoral Research Fellow at Opportunity Insights and the Stanford Institute for Economic Policy Research. I received my BA in Politics, Philosophy, and Economics from the Australian National University where I studied as a Tuckwell Scholar.
